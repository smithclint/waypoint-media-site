Terms & Conditions

Professional video production services for Waypoint Media. Please review these terms before booking your project.

Deliverables

Your package includes the final edited video(s) as described in your proposal. Unless otherwise noted, deliverables include:

  • Professionally edited video in HD format (or agreed resolution)
  • Licensed music (if applicable)
  • Logo intro/outro (if included)
  • 1 social media cut (if included)
  • Up to 1 free revision for small edits (see below)

Raw footage is not included unless specifically requested. If delivered, it is non-exclusive — you can use it, but I retain the right to showcase it for demos or portfolio use. (Exclusive-use licensing available on request.)
